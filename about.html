Hi I'm John LeBlanc the maker/creative. I'm from Fresno, California. I have a degree in Inderdisciplinary studies in Fine Arts and Natural Science & a minor in Biblical Studies from Liberty University online. I'm a husband a dad, an avid learner and DIY-er. My dream is to have a career helping people find joy and meaning, inspiring people to love them selves and the world around them, and to see all the creative potential they have. (I'm sure I'll figure that out someday.) In the mean time I like to make things that make people happy. From paintings that show how beautiful and fun the world is, to dinning room tables where friends and family can gather to enjoy meals, to the very meals my friends and family eat at my dinning table making things for people to enjoy brings me happiness. I hope you can find something here that makes your day better.
